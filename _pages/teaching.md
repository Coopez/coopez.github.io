As part of my PhD, I am assisting with the course [TEK5040 - Deep Learning for Autonomous Systems](https://www.uio.no/studier/emner/matnat/its/TEK5040/index-eng.html) at the University of Oslo.

Explaining concepts and guiding intuitions are very fun and interesting to me, extending to several aspects of academic work, such as outreach, communication, or writing. Naturally, my preference also makes teaching quite a rewarding experience. I am also a very strong believer into the idea of teaching guiding my own learning and understanding. In addition, I try to actively further my own understanding of teaching by attending several courses on the topic. 


I am actively supervising Master students! 
If you are a prospective student and interested in a thesis adjacent to my research topics (adjacent here in a loose sense; checkout the [Master's theses](/teaching/master-theses/) page), please reach out!
